INGREDIENTS TO MAKE A SANDWICH:

  • Bread Sandwich
  • 1/2 cup Onions
  • 1/2 cup Tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Cucumber
  • 1/2 cup Cabbage
  • 1/2 cup carrots
  • 1/4 cup Mayonnaise
  • Pepper Powder
  • Salt
  • Corriander leaves
  • Butter

    STEPS TO MAKE A SANDWICH:

  1. First of all Cut all the Tomatoes, Onions, Cabbage Cucumber.Carrot into small chopped pieces
  2. Take a Bowl and mix all the chopped pieces into it
  3. Add 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  4. Add 1/2 Pepper Powder
  5. Add some Salt & 2 table spoon Corriander leaves
  6. Mix it well all the ingredients
  7. Take 2 Breads and apply some butter and then add some green chutney into two Breads
  8. Add some veg stuffing that we prepare before into one of the bread piece
  9. ttach the another bread piece into Mixed bread piece

    PROCESS TO MAKE A SANDWICH:

  • Take A Grill pan and add 2 tea spoon butter and mix the butter in the pan
  • Take the bread pieces that we mixed the veg stuffing pieces
  • Close the lid gently and wait for some time for deep fried well of sandwich for both sides
  • keep an eye of sandwich when it reaches your desired level of crispness
  • Carefully remove your sandwich and serve it hot
